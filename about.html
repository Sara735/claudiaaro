Empathetic, Provocative, Transformative.

After earning a BSc in Nutrition and a MA in Public Health, I worked for over 20 years as a Senior Director for organizations in the health sector, building national partnerships and programs in service of a variety of communities. After I was diagnosed with and treated for breast cancer at the age of 41, I reevaluated my professional path and was inspired to shift my focus from serving communities to serving individuals.

I trained at the Coaching Training Institute, one of the most highly regarded coaching schools in the world, and became certified by the International Coaching Federation. I went on to train as a Mediator, with various accredited organizations and became a member of the ADR Institute of Ontario. I continue to sharpen my skills as a coach and mediator, intuiting what is unsaid, communicating directly and compassionately, supporting my clients through change and transformation.

I firmly believe that my methodology provides effective and critical tools that have a positive impact on the lives of my clients. I can coach and mediate in three languages (English, Spanish and Hebrew) and, having resided and studied in numerous countries, I am equipped to embrace and understand a variety of cultures and viewpoints, which enables me to work towards balanced strategies and resolutions.

Whether you’re facing the challenge of change or the discomfort of a situation that you know isn’t right, if you feel confused, stuck or are struggling to define and take the next step forward, don’t hesitate to contact me. The change of a lifetime awaits you.
